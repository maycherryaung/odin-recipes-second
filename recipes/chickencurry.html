Chicken Curry

Description

This is also one of the meals that I have made myself lazily while living alone. This is pretty simple yet very healthy and scrumptious.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Coconut milk
  • Onions and garlic
  • Coriander
  • Chinese seasoning powder mix

Steps

  1. Season the chicken.
  2. Chop the onions and garlic into small pieces.
  3. Heat up the frying pan and pour in cooking oil. When the oil is hot, put in the onion pieces.
  4. This is an important step as you will have to stir the onion pieces continuously on low heat.
  5. When the onion pieces turn golden, put in the chopped garlic pieces.
  6. When the garlic smell comes out, put in seasoned chicken and stir for about 5 minutes.
  7. Pour in the coconut milk and leave it on for some time. Add water if needed.
  8. Put in the seasoning powder mix and when the meat is soft, turn off the heat, put chopped coriander onto the curry and we have a lovely meal!
