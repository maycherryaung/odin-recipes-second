Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Instead of a full-blown korean recipe kimchi fried rice, I'm just going to share with you how I like to make it simply at home.This takes less than 20 minutes and it is really delicious and also nutritious. Let's dive in!

Ingredients

  • Ready-made Kimchi
  • Well-cooked rice
  • Minced pork
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables of your choice

Steps

  1. Heat up the frying pan for several seconds, then pour in a moderate amount of cooking oil.
  2. When the oil is hot, throw in minced pork, sprinkle salt onto it and stir thoroughly.
  3. Once the meat looks cooked on the outside, pour in some water and cover the pan.
  4. Once the water is gone, put in ready-made kimchi and stir.
  5. Put in the rice and stir further.
  6. Lastly, pour in the vegetables and stir until they are soft. Pour in some water if needed. Don't forget to sprinkle some salt.
  7. When the vegetables are soft, take out the fried rice and place on a plate.
  8. Make an omelette and put it over the rice. Now the meal is ready to be consumed!
