Fried Banana

Description

This is a traditional recipes in my country. We like to eat fried bananas as snacks. It is so easy to make this by yourself and it tastes amazing.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Cooking oil
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Peel the skin off of the bananas and put them on a plate. You can cut them into smaller pieces if you wish.
  2. Put the frying pan on the stove. When it gets slightly heated, pour in just a bit of cooking oil.
  3. When the oil is heated to a suitable temperature, just put the bananas in the pan.
  4. When the bananas are considerably fried, turn them onto the other side and fry again.
  5. When the bananas turn brown, you can take them out and put them on the plate.
  6. Just sprinkle some sugar onto the fried bananas and you can enjoy a delicious snack.
